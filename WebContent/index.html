ABOUT US


MARMARAS NAVIGATION LTD was established in 1976 and manages a modern fleet of thirty three bulkers with a total capacity of over 4.2 million deadweight and average age of 9.7 years. Through its focus to the Greek Flag and commitment to safe and environmentally responsible operations combined with high quality service, MARMARAS has gained the privilege to be considered as a leader in global shipping.
